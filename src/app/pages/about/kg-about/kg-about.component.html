Our Mission
" Innovating homes. "

At Kundan, we all come to work every day because we want to solve the biggest problem in house building. Everyone is guessing. House builders don’t know how to build, how to monetize them, or even what to pay price for the products. They aren’t sure which products and genres are the latest trend, and where to really spend their time (and money).

Throughout the history of business, people use relations to make more informed decisions. Our mission at Kundan is to make building houses economical and hassle free. Today we provide the most reliable insights in the industry. We want to make this house building solutions available to as many people as possible (not just the top 5%).
